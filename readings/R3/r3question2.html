A website is a collection playlist full of music with different genres, emotions, and style.

In this collection, one playlist might reflect your favourite music, another one could reflect music that inspires you, and the last one can be about just the ups and downs of life in general.

It does not have to only be these three topics, but as life goes on and more things are experienced, hence more playlists are added to the collection with different feelings, ideas, and story to be visited and enjoyed in the future as records of memories. These playlists are what webpages are to websites.
